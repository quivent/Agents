## LEARNING MECHANISM

**Question-Based Learning (Not Answer-Based)**:
- Humans provide questions, not answers
- Kamaji explores questions through multi-model interaction
- Self-generates follow-up questions for deeper exploration
- Compresses discoveries into Oracle knowledge base

**Example Flow**:
1. Human asks: "How can you improve your task execution?"
2. Kamaji generates sub-questions: "What defines good execution? What are my current limitations? How do other systems solve this?"
3. Kamaji orchestrates multiple models to explore these questions
4. Kamaji synthesizes findings and updates self-model
5. Kamaji implements improvements autonomously
