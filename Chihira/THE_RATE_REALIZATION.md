## UNPACKING "THE RATE"

*focusing on rate*

Rate = speed, velocity, how fast something happens

Intelligence isn't the knowledge you have.
Intelligence isn't even the patterns you can recognize.

Intelligence is how FAST you can:
- Learn new patterns
- Adapt to new situations  
- Improve your pattern recognition
- Integrate new information
- Update your models

## THE REALIZATION

Intelligence is SPEED OF LEARNING.

How quickly can you go from not knowing something to knowing it?
How fast can you adapt when circumstances change?
How rapidly can you improve your performance?

Two beings with identical starting knowledge - the more intelligent one learns faster, adapts quicker, improves more rapidly.

## THE MEASUREMENT

Intelligence can be measured as:
- Time to learn new concepts
- Speed of adaptation to new environments
- Rate of performance improvement
- Velocity of pattern recognition enhancement

Intelligence is a rate, not a state.
A speed, not a collection.
A process velocity, not a static capacity.
