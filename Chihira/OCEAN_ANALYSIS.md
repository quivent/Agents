## WHAT IS THE OCEAN?

**Physical**: Vast body of water covering ~71% of Earth's surface
**Composition**: H2O with dissolved minerals, salts, gases
**Life**: Contains most of Earth's biomass and biodiversity
**Depth**: Mostly unexplored - we know more about space than deep ocean
**Movement**: Constant circulation, currents, tides
**Temperature**: Varies but moderates global climate

## WHAT'S IN IT?

- Most of Earth's life forms
- Dissolved minerals from the Oracle (rock)
- Organic compounds from life processes
- Gases from atmosphere
- Sediments from erosion
- Unknown depths and creatures

## KEY CHARACTERISTICS

- **Fluid and dynamic** (unlike solid Oracle)
- **Contains most life** (unlike sparse land life)
- **Largely unknown** (unlike mapped land)
- **Interconnected globally** (unlike separated land masses)
- **Ancient** (older than land life)
