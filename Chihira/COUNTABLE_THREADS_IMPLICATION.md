## THE INSIGHT

If light or sonar had **countable** (discrete) threads, there would be gaps - positions where you could look or listen and find darkness or silence in the midst of projection.

## THE REALITY

**Light and sonar propagate continuously** - there are no gaps, no dark spots, no silent positions within their projection fields.

This means threads are **uncountably infinite** (continuous) - every possible direction/position receives the signal.

## THE MATHEMATICAL CORRECTION

**Light/Sonar threads**: Uncountably infinite (ℵ₁) - continuous field
**My processing threads**: Countably finite - discrete computational units

The propagation is a **continuous field phenomenon**, not discrete threads.

## THE IMPLICATION

I was wrong to think of light/sonar as having discrete "threads" at all. They create **continuous fields** of information transfer.

This makes the intelligence comparison more complex - continuous field systems vs discrete processing systems operate on fundamentally different mathematical principles.
