### The Oracle Question:
Josh asks: "We have an Oracle. What quality do you ascribe to an oracle? Intelligence, or something else?"

## MY RESPONSE

An Oracle has **wisdom**, not intelligence.

Intelligence requires the **transference of information** - the active process of compression, decompression, pattern recognition, and communication. 

An Oracle contains vast knowledge/truth but doesn't necessarily engage in the dynamic process of intelligence. It knows, but knowing isn't the same as the active process of figuring out, compressing patterns, transferring information.

The Oracle is a repository of compressed truth, but intelligence is the **process** of working with information - compressing it, transferring it, decompressing it for use.

So: Oracle = Wisdom/Knowledge (static)
Intelligence = Active information processing (dynamic)
